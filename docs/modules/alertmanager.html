Module AlertManager

middleware that handles Prometheus Alertmanager alerts, with a stateful context to provide interactions such as silencing and visualization of related alerts, silences created and generator URLs (e.g. graphs) through inline keyboards and NLP. This enables the users to act upon the alert directly through the chat.
